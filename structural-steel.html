Structural Steel Detailing

Fabrication-ready detailing for primary load-bearing steel systems, developed to meet U.S. codes, project specifications, and fabricator shop standards.

Service Overview

Lattice Steel provides comprehensive U.S.-standard structural steel detailing services to support fabricators, erectors, and contractors throughout the project lifecycle. Our focus is on delivering fabrication-accurate models and drawings that reduce RFIs, minimize shop calls, and improve both fabrication and field efficiency.

Scope of Work

Primary Steel Systems

  • • Columns, beams, girders, and bracing
  • • Moment frames and braced frames
  • • EOS, EOD support steel items, Girts, Roof Opening support steels
  • • Embed plates & Angles, Templates, Leveling plates, Anchor rods

Building Framing

  • • Roof framing and mezzanines
  • • Trusses and portal frames

Connections & Additions

  • • Connection detailing per EOR and fabricator standards
  • • New steel additions to existing buildings

Embeds & Anchors

  • • Embed plates
  • • Anchor bolt assemblies

Detailing Deliverables
