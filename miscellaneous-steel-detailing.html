Miscellaneous Steel Detailing

Accurate detailing of secondary steel elements supporting access, safety, architectural features, and equipment installation.

Service Overview

We provide comprehensive miscellaneous steel detailing services for secondary steel elements that support building access, safety, and functionality. Our detailing is fabrication-ready, site-coordinated, and aligned with U.S. shop and erection practices.

Access Systems

  • • Industrial, commercial, and egress stairs
  • • Ladders and cage ladders

Safety Components

  • • Handrails and guardrails
  • • Bollards and protective barriers

Platforms & Walkways

  • • Platforms, catwalks, and walkways
  • • Floor grating and checker plates

Supports

  • • Pipe supports
  • • Equipment and mechanical supports

Embeds & Anchors

  • • Embed plates
  • • Anchor bolt assemblies

Architectural Steel

  • • Brackets, lintels, frames
  • • Canopies and façade supports

Detailing Deliverables

  • Site-coordinated detailing
