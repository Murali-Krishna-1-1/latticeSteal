About Lattice Steel

Precision-driven steel detailing built on technology, quality assurance, and a deep understanding of U.S. fabrication practices.

Who We Are

Lattice Steel is a specialized steel detailing and support firm providing U.S.-standard structural and miscellaneous steel detailing, estimation, and independent QA review services to Fabrictors and Third party detailers

We work closely with fabricators, erectors, and contractors to deliver fabrication-ready models and drawings that reduce RFIs, minimize shop interruptions, and improve overall project efficiency.

An IT-Driven Approach to Steel Detailing

Model as Data

We treat every 3D model as a structured technical database, not just a visual representation. This allows greater control, accuracy, and consistency across drawings.

QA-First Mindset

Our background in professional quality assurance enables multi-level checking processes that identify issues early and reduce downstream revisions.

Fabrication Alignment

All detailing is developed in alignment with U.S. shop practices, erection constraints, and fabricator preferences.
