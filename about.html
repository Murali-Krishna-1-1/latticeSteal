Precision & Integrity

Who We Are

Lattice Steel is a specialized detailing firm supporting U.S. fabricators, erectors, and contractors. We see ourselves as an extension of your shop, delivering models and drawings that work in the real world, not just on screen.

Our goal is simple: Reduce RFIs, eliminate shop errors, and accelerate erection schedules through superior detailing accuracy.

IT-Driven Approach

Model as Data

We treat the 3D model as a master database. Drawings are just views of that data, ensuring 100% consistency.

QA Methodology

Multi-level independent checks prevent errors. We check geometry, standards, and constructability simultaneously.

Fabrication Focus

Every connection is detailed with the shop and field in mind. If it can't be built easily, we redesign it (with approval).
