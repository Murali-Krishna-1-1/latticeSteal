Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

Lattice Steel is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding the information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us or use our website:

  • Name, company name, email address, and phone number
  • Project-related information shared voluntarily (drawings, specs)
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type for website functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly for:

  • Responding to inquiries and providing requested quotes
  • Communicating about ongoing projects
  • Improving our service delivery

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Confidentiality

All project data, drawings, and documents shared with Lattice Steel are treated as strictly confidential. They are stored securely and accessed only by team members directly involved in your project.

4. Data Security

We implement robust administrative and technical security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
