Estimation & Material Take-Off

Accurate, model-based steel quantity take-offs and estimation support to reduce bidding risk and improve procurement confidence.

Service Overview

We provide accurate steel quantity take-offs and estimation modeling to support bidding, budgeting, and material planning. Our estimates are developed directly from Tekla Structures and SDS/2 models, ensuring consistency between quantities, fabrication drawings, and shop workflows.

Miscellaneous Steel

Bolts, Welds & Plates
