Estimation & Material Take-Off

Accurate, model-based steel quantity take-offs and estimation support to reduce bidding risk and improve procurement confidence.

Service Overview

We provide accurate steel quantity take-offs and estimation modeling to support bidding, budgeting, and material planning. Our estimates are developed directly from Tekla Structures and SDS/2 models, ensuring consistency between quantities, fabrication drawings, and shop workflows.

Scope of Work

Structural Steel

  • • Review structural and architectural drawings
  • • Perform steel quantity take-offs using Bluebeam and 3D modeling tools
  • • Identify primary, secondary, and miscellaneous steel elements
  • • Generate preliminary 3D steel models for visualization and validation
  • • Prepare material take-off (MTO) and weight reports
  • • Assist in budget pricing and bid preparation
  • • Provide clarification support during tender stages

Estimation Deliverables

  • Material Take-Off (MTO) reports
  • Weight summaries by member and assembly
  • Excel-based Bills of Quantities (BOQs)
  • Model-backed quantity validation
  • Consistency checks against drawings
  • CSV / Excel output formats

Software & Tools Used

Tekla Structures

Model-based quantity extraction and weight calculations directly from fabrication-ready models.

SDS/2

Estimation modeling aligned with shop drawings and fabrication workflows.

Excel / CSV

Clean, organized output formats for procurement, pricing, and review.

Blue Beam

Precise quantity take-offs and cost planning directly from project drawings.
