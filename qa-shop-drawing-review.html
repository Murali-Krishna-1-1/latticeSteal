QA & Shop Drawing Review

Independent quality assurance reviews that identify missing information, coordination issues, and constructability risks before fabrication.

Service Overview

We provide independent QA and shop drawing review services for steel fabricators to catch errors and coordination issues early. Our reviews help prevent shop interruptions, reduce RFIs, and minimize costly field delays by identifying issues before fabrication begins.

Scope of QA Review

Completeness Checks

  • • Missing dimensions and callouts
  • • Incomplete member or connection information

Material Verification

  • • Material grades and specifications
  • • Plate, bolt, and weld consistency

Connection Review

  • • Connection clarity and detailing logic
  • • Alignment with approved connection designs

Erection Feasibility

  • • Field access and clearance checks
  • • Logical erection sequencing

Code Compliance

  • • AISC and AWS compliance checks
  • • OSHA erection considerations

Coordination Review

  • • Drawing and model coordination issues
  • • Cross-discipline conflict identification

QA Review Deliverables

  • Marked-up shop drawings (PDF)
  • Clear, traceable QA comments
  • Consolidated issue lists
  • RFI-ready documentation
  • Revision tracking support
